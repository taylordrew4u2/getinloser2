# App Store Submission Metadata Template

## App Name
- **Name**: (Enter the name of your app as it will appear on the App Store. Maximum 30 characters.)

## Subtitle
- **Subtitle**: (Enter a short subtitle that highlights your app's value. Maximum 30 characters.)

## App Description
- **Description**: (Provide a detailed and engaging description of your app, its features, and benefits. Maximum 4000 characters.)

## Keywords
- **Keywords**: (List keywords separated by commas to help users find your app. Maximum 100 characters.)

## Support URL
- **Support URL**: (Provide a URL where users can get support for your app.)

## Marketing URL
- **Marketing URL**: (Provide a URL for your app's marketing website, if available.)

## Privacy Policy URL
- **Privacy Policy URL**: (Provide a URL to your app's privacy policy.)

## App Icon
- Ensure all required app icon sizes are included in the `AppIcon` asset catalog.

## Screenshots
- **Screenshots**: (Upload screenshots for all required device sizes. Include captions if necessary.)

## App Previews
- **App Previews**: (Optional. Upload short videos showcasing your app.)

## Category
- **Primary Category**: (Select the most relevant category for your app.)
- **Secondary Category**: (Optional. Select a secondary category if applicable.)

## Age Rating
- **Age Rating**: (Specify the appropriate age rating for your app.)

## App Privacy
- **Data Collection**: (Describe what data your app collects and how it is used.)
- **Privacy Choices**: (Describe any user privacy options available in your app.)

## In-App Purchases
- **In-App Purchases**: (List any in-app purchases, their names, and pricing.)

## Test Information
- **Demo Account**: (Provide demo account credentials for the App Review team, if required.)
- **Notes for Review**: (Add any additional notes for the App Review team.)

## Release Options
- **Release Type**: (Choose between manual release or automatic release after approval.)

## Localization
- **Languages**: (List the languages your app supports.)
- **Localized Metadata**: (Provide translations for the above fields if applicable.)
